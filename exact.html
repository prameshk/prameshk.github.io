Exact Algorithms For Hard Problems

This page is a personal experiment inspired by the excellent blog Property Testing Review. The PTR publishes paragraph-length summaries of new papers in property testing and sublinear-time algorithms as they appear on the arXiv on a monthly basis. To keep abreast of recent developments, I've endeavored to do the same for the field of exact (usually exponential-time) algorithms for hard problems. This blog was established in February 2021.
