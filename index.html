About me

I'm a PhD student at Columbia University advised by Rocco Servedio and Xi Chen. This is my personal webpage, and it's probably still under construction.

I'm broadly interested in chance, efficiency, and complex behavior. In research terms, that means I study a range of topics including randomization, complexity, algorithmic game theory and learning algorithms. In non-research terms, that means I like to explore the world and think about the way people living in it spend their time.
